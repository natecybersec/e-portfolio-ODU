About Me

  • 2001 // Sterling, Virginia.
  • The year I was born. I lived here until I was about 3 years old.
  • 2004 // Tirana, Albania.
  • My family and I moved to Albania, due to my parent's work. I don't remember much about Albania, probably because I was so young at the time.
  • 2006 // Manama, Bahrain.
  • We left Albania and moved to Bahrain. I also don't remember a whole lot about Bahrain, but I do recall having a favourite spot to get chicken.
  • 2008 // Amman, Jordan.
  • Moving to Jordan is the farthest back in my past that I can remember, albeit for very good reason. Jordan holds a lot of the building blocks of my life, along with being one of the greatest countries I've lived in. The school that I attended was called "ACS", which stands for "American Community School". I met one of my best friends who I still hang out with today, in Jordan at ACS. Also around this time is when I first remember discovering and being active on the internet. My favorite website was called YouTube, a video-hosting website that had came out a couple years earlier but was just starting to take off. I also remember frequenting flash game sites, which is where a passion for game development started to grow.
  • 2011 // Dhaka, Bangladesh.
  • Bangladesh is also a very important place to me. The school that I attended was extremely nice, and I made a lot of great friends while I was there. I also became increasingly interested in computers and how they were built. I made plans to build my own at some point.
  • 2014 // Winchester, Virginia.
  • Winchester has been my home since 2014. I graduated middle school and high school here, and hopefully soon, I'll graduate college here too.
